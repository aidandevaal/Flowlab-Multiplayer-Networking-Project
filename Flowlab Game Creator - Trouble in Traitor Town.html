How To Play

  • WASD to move
  • E to use pickaxe (weapon)
  • (If Traitor) - F to prove Traitor

Startup

  • 6 players must be fully connected for the game to begin.
  • Once 6 players have joined, each player must walk into a log.
  • Once you walk into a log, you will be given a role.
  • Once everyone has been given a role, the game may begin.
  • The red number in the top left is the Traitor count.
  • The green number in the top right is the Citizen count.
  • There is a 30 second timer before your pickaxe can be used.

The Main Idea

This is a party game, the Traitors must try and eliminate the Citizens and the Citizens have to figure out who the Traitors are and kill them first.

Worth Noting

  • It isn't guaranteed that there will be Traitors in every game
  • Be careful not to use your pickaxe too close to walls or it could bounce back and hit you
  • This game heavily relies on player cooperation, meaning no "Random Deathmatch" (killing with no decent reasoning) and avoiding grouping as much as possible
